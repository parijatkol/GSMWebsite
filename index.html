About Us

We believe in  Swach Bharat , Swabalambi Bharat (Clean India , Self-Reliant India)

Lt. Kamal Kumar Chatterjee was one of the founder member of our club, in the year 1952.

Lt. Kamal Kumar Chatterjee, Lt. Samir Chakroborty, Lt. Panna Lal Bose, Lt. Sunil Kr Banerjee, Mr. Sushil kr Ghosh, and may more formed a small club which was made of mud later they repair and renovated it with brick and  named  it “ SANGHA MITRA ”.

He was the only member who at the age of 78, again gave the idea of renovation and supported us to make a two storied building.


Gorabazar Sangha Mitra Club (Dum Dum Cantonment) , believes that self-reliance is the foundation of all social services. It has captured the initiative on several fronts without being dependent on government sponsorship or state-run schemes. Since last five years after saving some money from our Durga Puja Budget we started to do some Social Work. This differentiates the club from the NGOs. Charity, it is said, begins from home.

The club has promoted cleanliness through installation of plastic dustbins in the locality and also providing some training to all the local maid servants to throw garbage in the dustbin by the help of some awareness camp. Previously it was noted as all the walls of the locality was captured by the political groups however, now it is framed with wonderful caption and inspirable statements.
